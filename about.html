About PixiEdit

PixiEdit is a user-friendly online image editor designed to simplify the photo editing process for everyone—from casual users to creative professionals. With an intuitive interface and essential tools at your fingertips, PixiEdit makes it easy to enhance, transform, and personalize your images without the need for advanced software.

Whether you're looking to convert an image to grayscale, resize for social media, rotate or flip, or adjust brightness and contrast, PixiEdit provides fast, high-quality editing right in your browser. The platform supports popular formats like JPG, PNG, JPEG, and WEBP, ensuring compatibility and ease of use.

PixiEdit was built with accessibility in mind. There's no need to install anything — all editing happens securely in your browser, making it accessible across desktop and mobile devices. For users who create an account, advanced features unlock even more creative possibilities.

Our mission is simple: give users the power to express their creativity without barriers. Whether you're editing personal photos, creating visuals for work or school, or managing content for social media, PixiEdit helps you do it quickly and beautifully.
